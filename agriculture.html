Bachelor of Science in Agriculture

3-Year Undergraduate Program

A comprehensive three-year undergraduate program that teaches all the scientific methods and techniques related to agriculture. Students gain practical knowledge in crop production, soil science, agricultural engineering, and farm management.

Program Structure

  • Crop Production and Management
  • Soil Science and Agricultural Chemistry
  • Agricultural Engineering
  • Plant Protection
  • Agricultural Economics
  • Agricultural Extension
  • Animal Husbandry
  • Practical Field Work

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with 50% marks in natural sciences
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology and/or Mathematics as subjects
  • Valid entrance exam score

Career Opportunities

  • Agricultural Officer
  • Assistant Plantation Manager
  • Agricultural Researcher
  • Agricultural Development Officer
  • Agricultural Engineer
  • Business Development Manager
  • Marketing Manager
