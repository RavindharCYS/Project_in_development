Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch)

5-Year Professional Program

A comprehensive five-year undergraduate program that trains students in spatial planning, design, and construction. This professional degree program prepares students for careers in architecture and related fields.

Program Structure

  • Architectural Design
  • Building Construction and Materials
  • History of Architecture
  • Structural Design
  • Environmental Studies
  • Computer Applications in Architecture
  • Professional Practice
  • Urban Design and Planning

Eligibility Criteria

  • 12th PCM with minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) score with 80% or above
  • JEE Main Paper 2 score (for IITs and NITs)
  • Diploma holders must have completed 3-year diploma program

Career Opportunities

  • Architectural Designer
  • Urban Planner
  • Interior Designer
  • Construction Manager
  • Sustainable Design Consultant
  • Heritage Conservation Architect
  • Architectural Journalist

Specializations Available

  • Interior Design
  • Urban Planning
  • Landscape Architecture
  • Sustainable Architecture
  • Construction Management

Program Highlights

  • Expected 3% global employment growth over next 10 years
  • Starting salary range: 4-5 LPA
  • Multiple entrance exam options (NATA, JEE, CEED)
  • Practical training and internship opportunities
  • Industry-aligned curriculum

Bachelor of Planning (B.Plan)

4-Year Professional Program

A specialized undergraduate program focusing on urban, regional, and environmental planning. Students learn to develop sustainable solutions for urban development and spatial organization.

Program Structure

  • Urban Planning Principles
  • Regional Planning
  • Transportation Planning
  • Environmental Planning
  • GIS and Remote Sensing
  • Planning Legislation
  • Infrastructure Planning
  • Urban Design

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Mathematics as compulsory subject
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid JEE Main score
  • Entrance exam specific to planning institutes

Career Opportunities

  • Urban Planner
  • Regional Planner
  • Environmental Planner
  • Transport Planner
  • GIS Specialist
  • Planning Consultant

Program Highlights

  • Growing demand in smart city initiatives
  • Integrated approach to urban development
  • Focus on sustainable planning
  • Industry partnerships
  • Research opportunities

Master of Architecture (M.Arch)

2-Year Postgraduate Program

An advanced professional degree program designed for architectural license preparation. The program focuses on advanced design concepts, research, and specialized architectural practices.

Program Structure

  • Advanced Architectural Design
  • Research Methodology
  • Urban Design Theory
  • Sustainable Architecture
  • Digital Design and Fabrication
  • Conservation and Heritage
  • Professional Practice
  • Thesis Project

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Arch degree from accredited university
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid GATE score
  • Some universities accept CEED score

Career Opportunities

  • Project Manager
  • Project Architect
  • Landscape Architect
  • Design Architect
  • Professor
  • Landscape/Design Conservator

Specialization Areas

  • Urban Design
  • Sustainable Architecture
  • Heritage Conservation
  • Digital Architecture
  • Landscape Architecture

Program Highlights

  • Starting salary range: 5-7 LPA
  • Advanced research opportunities
  • Specialization options available
  • Industry-academia collaboration
  • International exposure
