Bachelor of Design in Fashion Design

4-Year Professional Program

A comprehensive program that provides the fundamentals of design and fashion for training competent and professional designers and managers. The course offers an integrative approach to design, developing versatile skills applicable across various design fields.

Program Structure

  • Fashion Design Fundamentals
  • Pattern Making and Garment Construction
  • Textile Science and Technology
  • Fashion Illustration and Digital Design
  • Fashion Merchandising and Marketing
  • Trend Forecasting and Analysis
  • Portfolio Development
  • Fashion Show Production

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Design aptitude test clearance
  • Portfolio submission
  • Personal interview

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Designer
  • Clothing Designer
  • Costume Designer
  • Fashion Show Coordinator
  • Stylist
  • Fashion Critic
  • Fashion Entrepreneur
  • Fashion Consultant

Program Highlights

  • Industry-integrated curriculum
  • Hands-on practical training
  • Fashion show experience
  • Industry internships
  • International exposure
  • State-of-the-art design labs
