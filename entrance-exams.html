Entrance Examinations Guide

Comprehensive information about various entrance examinations in India

Engineering Entrance Exams

JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination)

Description:

National level engineering entrance exam for admission to NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions.

Eligibility:

  • Passed Class 12th with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • Minimum 75% marks in Class 12th or be in top 20 percentile
  • No age limit for JEE Main

Important Dates:

Application Start December 2024
Exam Date January/April 2025
Learn More

TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions)

Description:

State-level engineering counselling for admission to engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu.

Eligibility:

  • Passed HSC or equivalent with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • Tamil Nadu state domicile

Important Dates:

Registration Start June 2025
Counselling July-August 2025
Learn More

Medical Entrance Exams

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

Description:

National level medical entrance exam for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, and other medical courses.

Eligibility:

  • Passed Class 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB (40% for reserved categories)
  • Age limit: 17-25 years

Important Dates:

Application Start March 2025
Exam Date May 2025
Learn More

Management Entrance Exams

CAT (Common Admission Test)

Description:

National level management entrance exam for admission to IIMs and other top B-schools.

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Final year students can also apply
  • No specific age limit

Important Dates:

Registration Start August 2025
Exam Date November 2025
