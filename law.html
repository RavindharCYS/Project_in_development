BA LLB (Bachelor of Arts + Bachelor of Legislative Law)

A five-year integrated program combining arts and law education. This comprehensive course provides students with a strong foundation in both legal studies and liberal arts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 from recognized board
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • CLAT/LSAT/Law entrance qualification
  • Age: Below 20 years for general category

Career Opportunities

  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Civil Lawyer
  • Legal Advisor
  • Public Prosecutor
  • Legal Journalist

BBA LLB (Bachelor of Business Administration + Bachelor of Legislative Law)

A five-year integrated program combining business administration and law. Perfect for students interested in corporate law and business legal affairs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with minimum 50% marks
  • Valid entrance exam score
  • Age limit as per regulatory bodies
  • English proficiency

Career Opportunities

  • Corporate Legal Counsel
  • Business Law Consultant
  • Mergers & Acquisitions Specialist
  • Investment Banking Legal Advisor
  • Intellectual Property Lawyer

B.COM LLB (Bachelor of Commerce + Bachelor of Legislative Law)

A five-year integrated program combining commerce and law education. Ideal for students interested in taxation law, commercial law, and financial regulations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Commerce/Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam qualification
  • Age criteria as per guidelines

Career Opportunities

  • Tax Lawyer
  • Financial Law Consultant
  • Banking Law Expert
  • Commercial Law Specialist
  • Corporate Finance Lawyer
