BBA - Bachelor of Business Administration

A 3-year Undergraduate Degree in the Management field that develops leadership and managerial skills. Students gain comprehensive understanding of business operations and management methodologies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with minimum 50% marks in any stream
  • Entrance exams: SET, CUET, IPM Aptitude Test, IPMAT, IPU CAT
  • Admission based on combined entrance and board exam scores

Career Opportunities

  • HR Professional
  • Marketing Executive/Manager
  • Sales Manager
  • Research & Development Manager
  • Business Development Executive
  • Brand Manager
