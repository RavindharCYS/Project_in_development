MBBS Program

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery

Program Overview

The MBBS program is a comprehensive medical education spanning 5.5 years, designed to create competent medical professionals. The curriculum combines intensive theoretical study with practical clinical experience, preparing graduates for successful careers in medicine.

Duration: 5.5 years (Including Internship)

Program Type: Undergraduate Medical Degree

Career Outcomes: Physicians, Surgeons, Medical Officers

Why Choose MBBS in India?

  • World-Class Education: Access to premier medical institutions with internationally recognized standards and experienced faculty.
  • Affordable Excellence: High-quality medical education at a fraction of international costs.
  • Diverse Learning Environment: Exposure to varied medical cases and clinical scenarios.
  • Modern Infrastructure: State-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical equipment.
  • Global Recognition: Indian medical degrees are widely accepted internationally.

Eligibility Requirements

Basic Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • 12th grade completion with Science stream (PCB)
  • Qualifying marks: 50% (General), 40% (SC/ST/OBC), 45% (PWD)
  • NEET qualification mandatory
