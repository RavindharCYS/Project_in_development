MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery

A comprehensive 5.5-year program focusing on theoretical instruction and hands-on clinical internships, preparing students for excellence in medical practice.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of 12th standard with Science stream
  • Minimum 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • NEET qualification
  • Minimum age: 17 years

Career Scope

  • Clinical Practice in Hospitals
  • Specialized Medical Fields
  • Medical Research
  • Healthcare Administration
  • Medical Education

BDS - Bachelor of Dental Surgery

A comprehensive 4-year program followed by one year of mandatory internship, focusing on dental sciences and oral healthcare.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Successful completion of 12th standard examination
  • Science stream with English, Biology, Physics, and Chemistry
  • Minimum 50% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • NEET qualification

Career Scope

  • Private dental practices
  • Dental clinics and hospitals
  • Specializations (Orthodontics, Oral Surgery)
  • Dental research
  • Academic institutions
  • Public health dentistry

BHMS - Bachelor of Homeopathy Medicine and Surgery

A 5.5-year program with 4.5 years of academic learning and one year of internship in homeopathic medicine.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age of 17 years
  • 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in board examination
  • NEET qualification

Career Scope

  • Homeopathic pharmacy
  • Infertility and sex specialist
  • Psychiatry
  • Skin specialist
  • Pediatrics

BAMS - Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery

A 5.5-year program focusing on Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery for treating human ailments through traditional Indian medicine.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Duration: 5.5 years
  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • Passed Class 12 with minimum 50% marks (general category)
  • NEET qualification

Career Scope

  • Government healthcare centers
  • Private clinics
  • Wellness resorts
  • Research institutions
  • International opportunities in Ayurveda

BUMS - Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery

An AYUSH program rooted in Unani methods of medical treatment, providing a unique and holistic perspective on healthcare.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Duration: 5.5 years
  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • Class 12 completion with 50% marks (UR category)
  • NEET UG qualification

Career Scope

  • Government healthcare facilities
  • Private clinics
  • Wellness centers
  • Research institutions
  • International Unani practice

BSMS - Bachelor of Siddha Medicine and Surgery

A captivating AYUSH course that combines traditional healing with modern medical science.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Duration: 5.5 years
  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • Class 12 completion with science background
  • NEET exam qualification

Career Scope

  • Government healthcare facilities
  • Private practices
  • Research institutions
  • International opportunities
  • Academic institutions

B.V.Sc & AH - Bachelor of Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry

A 5-year program focusing on understanding and treating diseases of animals and birds, with excellent job prospects.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of Class 12
  • Science stream background
  • Relevant entrance exam qualification
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB

Career Scope

  • Public sector veterinary services
  • Private veterinary clinics
  • Animal research facilities
  • Wildlife conservation
  • Animal husbandry departments
