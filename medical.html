Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

BDS is a 5-year professional degree program including one year of compulsory internship. The program provides comprehensive training in dental sciences, oral healthcare, and surgical procedures.

Reasons to Pursue BDS

  • High Demand: Growing awareness of dental health has created substantial career opportunities.
  • Modern Facilities: Access to advanced dental equipment and latest technologies.
  • Diverse Opportunities: Options in private practice, hospitals, research, and academics.
  • Global Recognition: Indian dental degrees are widely accepted internationally.

Eligibility

  • Minimum 17 years of age
  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB (45% for SC/ST)
  • NEET qualification mandatory

Career Prospects

Private Practice

Establish your own dental clinic

Hospital Career

Work in government or private hospitals

Academic Career

Teach in dental colleges

Research

Pursue research in dental sciences

Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS)

BHMS is a 5.5-year program that focuses on homeopathic medicine and holistic healing approaches. The program combines traditional homeopathic principles with modern medical knowledge.

Reasons to Pursue BHMS

  • Natural Healing: Focus on natural remedies and holistic treatment approaches.
  • Growing Acceptance: Increasing global recognition of alternative medicine.
  • Minimal Side Effects: Treatment methods with fewer adverse effects.
  • Private Practice Potential: Opportunity to establish individual clinics.

Eligibility

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB
  • NEET qualification required
  • Age limit as per regulatory guidelines

Career Prospects

Private clinic or hospital practice

Research

Homeopathic research and development

Government Sector

Government hospitals and healthcare centers

Academia

Teaching in homeopathic colleges
