B.Sc Nursing

A comprehensive undergraduate program that enables students to serve humanity through medical treatment. The program covers essential topics in healthcare including physiology, anatomy, biochemistry, nutrition, dietetics, and psychology.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Age: 17 years minimum
  • Valid entrance exam score (as applicable)

Career Opportunities

  • Staff Nurse in Hospitals
  • Community Health Nurse
  • School Health Nurse
  • Industrial Nurse
  • Private Practice

M.Sc Nursing

An advanced program focusing on specialized nursing care and research. Students can specialize in various fields like Medical-Surgical Nursing, Pediatric Nursing, or Psychiatric Nursing.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Sc Nursing with minimum 55% marks
  • Registered with State Nursing Council
  • One year work experience
  • Entrance exam qualification

Career Opportunities

  • Nursing Educator
  • Clinical Specialist
  • Nursing Administrator
  • Research Nurse
  • Nursing Supervisor

Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery

A three-year program providing fundamental nursing education and practical training in general nursing and midwifery care.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum 40% aggregate marks
  • Age: 17-35 years
  • Selection through merit/entrance test

Career Opportunities

  • Staff Nurse in Hospitals
  • Community Health Worker
  • Maternal Care Specialist
  • Primary Healthcare Centers
  • Private Clinics
