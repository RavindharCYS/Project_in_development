B.Sc Anesthesia Technology

A comprehensive program designed to train students in the principles and practices of anesthesia technology, preparing them for crucial roles in operating rooms and surgical facilities. This program combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills in modern anesthesia techniques.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST)
  • Age: 17 years minimum
  • Valid entrance exam score (as applicable)

Career Scope

  • Operating Room Technologist
  • Anesthesia Technical Specialist
  • Critical Care Units
  • Emergency Departments
  • Research Institutions

B.Sc Cardiac Care Technology

A specialized program focusing on cardiovascular technology and patient care. Students learn about cardiac monitoring, ECG technology, and cardiac catheterization procedures, preparing them for vital roles in cardiac care units.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid entrance exam qualification
  • Age: 17 years minimum

Career Scope

  • Cardiac Care Units
  • Catheterization Laboratories
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation Centers
  • Research Institutions
  • Medical Device Companies

B.Sc Imaging Technology

This program trains students in various medical imaging techniques including X-ray, CT scan, MRI, and ultrasound. Students learn to operate sophisticated imaging equipment and assist in diagnostic procedures.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam clearance
  • Basic computer knowledge

Career Scope

  • Radiography Technologist
  • MRI Technician
  • CT Scan Specialist
  • Ultrasound Technician
  • Diagnostic Imaging Centers

B.Sc Medical Laboratory Technology

A program that trains students in laboratory techniques and procedures essential for medical diagnosis. Students learn about blood banking, clinical biochemistry, microbiology, and pathology testing.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Science stream
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Biology as a mandatory subject
  • Entrance test qualification

Career Scope

  • Medical Laboratories
  • Blood Banks
  • Research Laboratories
  • Diagnostic Centers
  • Healthcare Facilities

B.Sc Perfusion Technology

This specialized program focuses on the science of operating heart-lung machines during cardiac surgery. Students learn about cardiopulmonary bypass techniques and life support systems.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with PCB subjects
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam qualification
  • Good physical and mental health

Career Scope

  • Cardiac Surgery Centers
  • Cardiovascular Hospitals
  • Research Institutions
  • Medical Equipment Companies
  • Teaching Institutions

This program prepares students for emergency medical services and trauma care. Students learn about emergency response, critical care, and disaster management.

Career Scope

Bachelor of Occupational Therapy (BOT)

A program that focuses on helping patients develop, recover, and improve their ability to perform daily activities. Students learn about therapeutic techniques and rehabilitation procedures.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam qualification
  • Good communication skills

Career Scope

  • Rehabilitation Centers
  • Hospitals and Clinics
  • Special Education Schools
  • Home Healthcare Services
  • Private Practice
