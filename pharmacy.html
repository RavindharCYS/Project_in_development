Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

A 4-year UG Degree Program in the Pharmacy field that provides both academic and practical training in the pharmaceutical field. Students learn about drug development, pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmacology, and healthcare management.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects
  • Mathematics/Biology/Biotechnology/Computer Science as optional
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks in PCM/PCB
  • Valid NEET or state-level entrance exam score

Career Opportunities

  • Hospital Pharmacist
  • Own Pharmacy Business
  • Sales & Marketing in Pharmaceutical Companies
  • Research & Development
  • Drug Control Administration
  • Quality & Assurance Manager

Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm)

A 2-year postgraduate program offering specialization in various branches of pharmacy such as Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, and Pharmaceutical Analysis.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Pharm degree with minimum 55% marks
  • Valid GPAT score
  • State level PG entrance exam qualification

Career Opportunities

  • Research Scientist
  • Drug Safety Associate
  • Clinical Research Coordinator
  • Academic Positions
  • Pharmaceutical Consultant

Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm D)

A 6-year doctoral program focusing on clinical pharmacy practice and patient care. The program includes extensive clinical training and hospital internships.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid entrance exam score
  • B.Pharm graduates can enter directly into 4th year

Career Opportunities

  • Clinical Pharmacist
  • Hospital Drug Information Specialist
  • Clinical Research Associate
  • Regulatory Affairs Officer
  • Healthcare Consultant

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm)

A 2-year diploma program providing basic knowledge of pharmacy practice and pharmaceutical sciences. Ideal for those looking to enter the pharmacy profession quickly.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Age: 17 years minimum
  • State level diploma entrance exam

Career Opportunities

  • Community Pharmacist
  • Hospital Pharmacy Assistant
  • Pharmaceutical Sales Representative
  • Drug Store Manager
  • Manufacturing Unit Assistant
