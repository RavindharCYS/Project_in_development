Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

A comprehensive 4.5-year UG degree program in Physiotherapy with an additional 6-month internship program. The course focuses on rehabilitation techniques, therapeutic exercises, and physical medicine.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Biology/Life Science as core subject
  • Minimum 50% marks in each subject (60% in some colleges)
  • Physics, Chemistry as mandatory subjects
  • Valid entrance exam score (as applicable)

Career Opportunities

  • Own Private Physiotherapy Practice
  • Health & Fitness Centre Professional
  • Rehabilitation & Recovery Tools Manufacturing
  • Employ in Boarding School as Physiotherapist
  • Sports Team Physiotherapist

Diploma in Physiotherapy (DPT)

A 2-year diploma program providing foundational knowledge in physiotherapy techniques and rehabilitation practices. Ideal for quick entry into the physiotherapy profession.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Science stream
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • Biology as a mandatory subject
  • State-level diploma entrance exam

Career Opportunities

  • Assistant Physiotherapist
  • Rehabilitation Centre Assistant
  • Fitness Centre Therapist
  • Sports Club Therapist
  • Clinical Setup Assistant

B.Sc in Physiotherapy

A 3-year bachelor's program focusing on scientific principles of physiotherapy, human anatomy, and rehabilitation techniques. Includes practical training and clinical exposure.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam qualification
  • Age: 17 years minimum

Career Opportunities

  • Clinical Physiotherapist
  • Research Assistant
  • Healthcare Facility Therapist
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Sports Physiotherapist

Master of Physiotherapy (MPT)

A 2-year postgraduate program offering advanced specialization in various areas of physiotherapy such as Sports, Neurology, Orthopedics, and Cardiopulmonary rehabilitation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • BPT or equivalent degree
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks
  • Completion of internship
  • Entrance exam qualification

Career Opportunities

  • Senior Physiotherapist
  • Academic Professor
  • Research Scientist
  • Clinical Specialist
  • Rehabilitation Center Head
