Integrated M.Sc. Biotechnology

5-Year Integrated Program

A comprehensive 5-year program that integrates bachelor's and master's level education in biotechnology. The program focuses on molecular biology, genetic engineering, agricultural biotechnology, and bioprocess technology, preparing students for research and industry roles.

Program Structure

  • Molecular Biology and Genetics
  • Biochemistry and Cell Biology
  • Microbiology and Immunology
  • Bioprocess Technology
  • Bioinformatics and Computational Biology
  • Industrial Biotechnology
  • Research Methodology and Biostatistics

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with minimum 60% marks from recognized board
  • PCB/PCM stream in higher secondary
  • Valid entrance exam score (JEE/NEET/University specific)
  • Merit-based admission process

Career Opportunities

  • Research Scientist in R&D Organizations
  • Biotechnology Consultant
  • Quality Control Manager
  • Clinical Research Associate
  • Bioinformatics Specialist
  • Academic Researcher
